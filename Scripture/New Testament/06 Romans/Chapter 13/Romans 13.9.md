[Romans 13:9](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/nt/rom/13?lang=eng&id=p9#p9) For this, Thou shalt not commit adultery, Thou shalt not kill, Thou shalt not steal, Thou shalt not bear false witness, Thou shalt not covet; and if there be any other commandment, it is briefly comprehended in this saying, namely, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.
