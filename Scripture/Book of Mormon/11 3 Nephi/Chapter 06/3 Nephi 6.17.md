[3 Nephi 6:17](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/6?lang=eng&id=p17#p17) And thus, in the commencement of the thirtieth year--the people having been delivered up for the space of a long time to be carried about by the temptations of the devil whithersoever he desired to carry them, and to do whatsoever iniquity he desired they should--and thus in the commencement of this, the thirtieth year, they were in a state of awful wickedness.
