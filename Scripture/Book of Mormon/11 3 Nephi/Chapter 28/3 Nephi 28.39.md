[3 Nephi 28:39](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/3-ne/28?lang=eng&id=p39#p39) Now this change was not equal to that which shall take place at the last day; but there was a change wrought upon them, insomuch that Satan could have no power over them, that he could not tempt them; and they were sanctified in the flesh, that they were holy, and that the powers of the earth could not hold them.
