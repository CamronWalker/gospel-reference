[Words of Mormon 1:3](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/w-of-m/1?lang=eng&id=p3#p3) And now, I speak somewhat concerning that which I have written; for after I had made an abridgment from the plates of Nephi, down to the reign of this king Benjamin, of whom Amaleki spake, I searched among the records which had been delivered into my hands, and I found these plates, which contained this small account of the prophets, from Jacob down to the reign of this king Benjamin, and also many of the words of Nephi.
