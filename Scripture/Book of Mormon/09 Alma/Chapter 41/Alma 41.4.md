[Alma 41:4](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/41?lang=eng&id=p4#p4) And if their works are evil they shall be restored unto them for evil. Therefore, all things shall be restored to their proper order, every thing to its natural frame--mortality raised to immortality, corruption to incorruption--raised to endless happiness to inherit the kingdom of God, or to endless misery to inherit the kingdom of the devil, the one on one hand, the other on the other--
