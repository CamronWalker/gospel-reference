[Alma 25:6](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/25?lang=eng&id=p6#p6) For many of them, after having suffered much loss and so many afflictions, began to be stirred up in remembrance of the words which Aaron and his brethren had preached to them in their land; therefore they began to disbelieve the traditions of their fathers, and to believe in the Lord, and that he gave great power unto the Nephites; and thus there were many of them converted in the wilderness.
