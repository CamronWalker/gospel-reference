[Alma 47:5](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/47?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) And now he had got the command of those parts of the Lamanites who were in favor of the king; and he sought to gain favor of those who were not obedient; therefore he went forward to the place which was called Onidah, for thither had all the Lamanites fled; for they discovered the army coming, and, supposing that they were coming to destroy them, therefore they fled to Onidah, to the place of arms.
