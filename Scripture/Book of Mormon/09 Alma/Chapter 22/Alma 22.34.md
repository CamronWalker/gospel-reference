[Alma 22:34](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/alma/22?lang=eng&id=p34#p34) Therefore the Lamanites could have no more possessions only in the land of Nephi, and the wilderness round about. Now this was wisdom in the Nephites--as the Lamanites were an enemy to them, they would not suffer their afflictions on every hand, and also that they might have a country whither they might flee, according to their desires.
