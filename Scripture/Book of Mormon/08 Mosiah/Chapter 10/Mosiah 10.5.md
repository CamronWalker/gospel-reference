[Mosiah 10:5](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/10?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) And I did cause that the women should spin, and toil, and work, and work all manner of fine linen, yea, and cloth of every kind, that we might clothe our nakedness; and thus we did prosper in the land--thus we did have continual peace in the land for the space of twenty and two years.
