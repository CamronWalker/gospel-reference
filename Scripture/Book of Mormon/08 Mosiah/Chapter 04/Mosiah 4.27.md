[Mosiah 4:27](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/mosiah/4?lang=eng&id=p27#p27) And see that all these things are done in wisdom and order; for it is not requisite that a man should run faster than he has strength. And again, it is expedient that he should be diligent, that thereby he might win the prize; therefore, all things must be done in order.
