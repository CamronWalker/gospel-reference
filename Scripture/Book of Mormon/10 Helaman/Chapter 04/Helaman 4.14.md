[Helaman 4:14](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/bofm/hel/4?lang=eng&id=p14#p14) But behold, Moronihah did preach many things unto the people because of their iniquity, and also Nephi and Lehi, who were the sons of Helaman, did preach many things unto the people, yea, and did prophesy many things unto them concerning their iniquities, and what should come unto them if they did not repent of their sins.
