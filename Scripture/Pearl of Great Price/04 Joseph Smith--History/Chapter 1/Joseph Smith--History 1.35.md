[Joseph Smith--History 1:35](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/js-h/1?lang=eng&id=p35#p35) Also, that there were two stones in silver bows--and these stones, fastened to a breastplate, constituted what is called the Urim and Thummim--deposited with the plates; and the possession and use of these stones were what constituted "seers" in ancient or former times; and that God had prepared them for the purpose of translating the book.
