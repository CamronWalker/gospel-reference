[Joseph Smith--History 1:32](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/js-h/1?lang=eng&id=p32#p32) Not only was his robe exceedingly white, but his whole person was glorious beyond description, and his countenance truly like lightning. The room was exceedingly light, but not so very bright as immediately around his person. When I first looked upon him, I was afraid; but the fear soon left me.
