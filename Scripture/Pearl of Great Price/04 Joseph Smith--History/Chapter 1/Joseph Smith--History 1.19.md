[Joseph Smith--History 1:19](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/js-h/1?lang=eng&id=p19#p19) I was answered that I must join none of them, for they were all wrong; and the Personage who addressed me said that all their creeds were an abomination in his sight; that those professors were all corrupt; that: "they draw near to me with their lips, but their hearts are far from me, they teach for doctrines the commandments of men, having a form of godliness, but they deny the power thereof."
