[Joseph Smith--History 1:14](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/js-h/1?lang=eng&id=p14#p14) So, in accordance with this, my determination to ask of God, I retired to the woods to make the attempt. It was on the morning of a beautiful, clear day, early in the spring of eighteen hundred and twenty. It was the first time in my life that I had made such an attempt, for amidst all my anxieties I had never as yet made the attempt to pray vocally.
