[Joseph Smith--History 1:55](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/js-h/1?lang=eng&id=p55#p55) As my father's worldly circumstances were very limited, we were under the necessity of laboring with our hands, hiring out by day's work and otherwise, as we could get opportunity. Sometimes we were at home, and sometimes abroad, and by continuous labor were enabled to get a comfortable maintenance.
