[The Articles of Faith 1:5](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/a-of-f/1?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) We believe that a man must be called of God, by prophecy, and by the laying on of hands by those who are in authority, to preach the Gospel and administer in the ordinances thereof.
