[The Articles of Faith 1:10](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/pgp/a-of-f/1?lang=eng&id=p10#p10) We believe in the literal gathering of Israel and in the restoration of the Ten Tribes; that Zion (the New Jerusalem) will be built upon the American continent; that Christ will reign personally upon the earth; and, that the earth will be renewed and receive its paradisiacal glory.
