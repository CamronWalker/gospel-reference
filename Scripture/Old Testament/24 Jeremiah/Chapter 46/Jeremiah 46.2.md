[Jeremiah 46:2](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/jer/46?lang=eng&id=p2#p2) Against Egypt, against the army of Pharaoh-necho king of Egypt, which was by the river Euphrates in Carchemish, which Nebuchadrezzar king of Babylon smote in the fourth year of Jehoiakim the son of Josiah king of Judah.
