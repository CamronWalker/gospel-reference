[Numbers 9:14](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/num/9?lang=eng&id=p14#p14) And if a stranger shall sojourn among you, and will keep the passover unto the LORD; according to the ordinance of the passover, and according to the manner thereof, so shall he do: ye shall have one ordinance, both for the stranger, and for him that was born in the land.
