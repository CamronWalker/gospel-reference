[2 Chronicles 35:8](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/2-chr/35?lang=eng&id=p8#p8) And his princes gave willingly unto the people, to the priests, and to the Levites: Hilkiah and Zechariah and Jehiel, rulers of the house of God, gave unto the priests for the passover offerings two thousand and six hundred small cattle, and three hundred oxen.
