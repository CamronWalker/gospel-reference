[2 Samuel 16:2](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/ot/2-sam/16?lang=eng&id=p2#p2) And the king said unto Ziba, What meanest thou by these?  And Ziba said, The asses be for the king's household to ride on; and the bread and summer fruit for the young men to eat; and the wine, that such as be faint in the wilderness may drink.
