[D&C 42:92](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/42?lang=eng&id=p92#p92) If any shall offend in secret, he or she shall be rebuked in secret, that he or she may have opportunity to confess in secret to him or her whom he or she has offended, and to God, that the church may not speak reproachfully of him or her.
