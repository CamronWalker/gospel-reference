[D&C 124:93](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/124?lang=eng&id=p93#p93) That whoever he blesses shall be blessed, and whoever he curses shall be cursed; that whatsoever he shall bind on earth shall be bound in heaven; and whatsoever he shall loose on earth shall be loosed in heaven.
