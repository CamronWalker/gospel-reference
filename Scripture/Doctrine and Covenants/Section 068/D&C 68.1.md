[D&C 68:1](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/68?lang=eng&id=p1#p1) MY servant, Orson Hyde, was called by his ordination to proclaim the everlasting gospel, by the Spirit of the living God, from people to people, and from land to land, in the congregations of the wicked, in their synagogues, reasoning with and expounding all scriptures unto them.
