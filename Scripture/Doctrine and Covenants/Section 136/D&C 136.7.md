[D&C 136:7](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/136?lang=eng&id=p7#p7) Let each company, with their captains and presidents, decide how many can go next spring; then choose out a sufficient number of able-bodied and expert men, to take teams, seeds, and farming utensils, to go as pioneers to prepare for putting in spring crops.
