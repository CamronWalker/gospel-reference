[D&C 81:3](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/81?lang=eng&id=p3#p3) Therefore, verily I acknowledge him and will bless him, and also thee, inasmuch as thou art faithful in counsel, in the office which I have appointed unto you, in prayer always, vocally and in thy heart, in public and in private, also in thy ministry in proclaiming the gospel in the land of the living, and among thy brethren.
