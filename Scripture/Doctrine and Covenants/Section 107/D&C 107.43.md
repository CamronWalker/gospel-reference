[D&C 107:43](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p43#p43) Because he (Seth) was a perfect man, and his likeness was the express likeness of his father, insomuch that he seemed to be like unto his father in all things, and could be distinguished from him only by his age.
