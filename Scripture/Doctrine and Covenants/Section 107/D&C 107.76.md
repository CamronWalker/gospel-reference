[D&C 107:76](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p76#p76) But a literal descendant of Aaron has a legal right to the presidency of this priesthood, to the keys of this ministry, to act in the office of bishop independently, without counselors, except in a case where a President of the High Priesthood, after the order of Melchizedek, is tried, to sit as a judge in Israel.
