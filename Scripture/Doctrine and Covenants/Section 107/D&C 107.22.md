[D&C 107:22](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/107?lang=eng&id=p22#p22) Of the Melchizedek Priesthood, three Presiding High Priests, chosen by the body, appointed and ordained to that office, and upheld by the confidence, faith, and prayer of the church, form a quorum of the Presidency of the Church.
