[D&C 102:27](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/102?lang=eng&id=p27#p27) Should the parties or either of them be dissatisfied with the decision of said council, they may appeal to the high council of the seat of the First Presidency of the Church, and have a re-hearing, which case shall there be conducted, according to the former pattern written, as though no such decision had been made.
