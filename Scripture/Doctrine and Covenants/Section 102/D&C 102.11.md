[D&C 102:11](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/102?lang=eng&id=p11#p11) And in case of the absence of one or both of those who are appointed to assist him, he has power to preside over the council without an assistant; and in case he himself is absent, the other presidents have power to preside in his stead, both or either of them.
