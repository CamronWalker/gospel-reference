[D&C 102:12](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/102?lang=eng&id=p12#p12) Whenever a high council of the church of Christ is regularly organized, according to the foregoing pattern, it shall be the duty of the twelve councilors to cast lots by numbers, and thereby ascertain who of the twelve shall speak first, commencing with number one and so in succession to number twelve.
