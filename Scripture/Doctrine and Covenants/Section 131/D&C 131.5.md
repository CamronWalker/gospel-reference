[D&C 131:5](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/131?lang=eng&id=p5#p5) (May 17th, 1843.) The more sure word of prophecy means a man's knowing that he is sealed up unto eternal life, by revelation and the spirit of prophecy, through the power of the Holy Priesthood.
