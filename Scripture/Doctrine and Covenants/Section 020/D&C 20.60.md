[D&C 20:60](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/20?lang=eng&id=p60#p60) Every elder, priest, teacher, or deacon is to be ordained according to the gifts and callings of God unto him; and he is to be ordained by the power of the Holy Ghost, which is in the one who ordains him.
