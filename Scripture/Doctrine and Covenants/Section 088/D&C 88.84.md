[D&C 88:84](https://churchofjesuschrist.org/study/scriptures/dc-testament/dc/88?lang=eng&id=p84#p84) Therefore, tarry ye, and labor diligently, that you may be perfected in your ministry to go forth among the Gentiles for the last time, as many as the mouth of the Lord shall name, to bind up the law and seal up the testimony, and to prepare the saints for the hour of judgment which is to come;
